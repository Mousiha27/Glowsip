A wellness brand rooted in nature

✨

Brand Guidelines

How GlowSip shows up.

Clear rules ensure the brand stays consistent and trustworthy across every touchpoint — from packaging to Instagram to business cards.

🎨
Colour Usage
Forest Green is the primary brand colour. Use it on packaging, headlines, and CTAs. Leaf Green adds energy as an accent. Never use both greens at equal weight — let one lead.
✓ Forest Green on cream backgrounds
✓ White logo on deep green
✗ Logo on busy photographic backgrounds
✗ Mix with off-brand colours
🔤
Typography Rules
Recoleta Alt exclusively for brand name and major headings. Blogger for all body copy, labels, and captions. Never set the brand name in Blogger, and never use Recoleta for long-form body text.
✓ Recoleta Alt for "GlowSip" wordmark
✓ Blogger at 14–16px for body
✗ Condensed or stretched type
✗ More than 2 typefaces at once
📐
Logo Clearspace
Always maintain a minimum clearspace equal to the height of the "G" in GlowSip around all sides of the logo. Never crowd the logo with other elements, borders, or text.
✓ Use provided logo files only
✓ Scale proportionally always
✗ Stretch or distort the logo
✗ Recreate or alter the logomark
📱
Social Media
Profile icon: use the circular logomark on Forest Green background. Feed imagery should feature natural textures, herbs, steam, and warm-lit cups. Maintain consistent filter temperature across posts.
✓ Warm, natural lighting
✓ Cream + green colour story
✗ Cold blue-toned imagery
✗ Busy, cluttered compositions
📦
Packaging Tone
Lead with benefits (Stress Relief, Better Sleep) before features. Use warm, reassuring language. Avoid clinical or pharmaceutical-sounding copy. The brand voice is: knowledgeable but gentle, natural but modern.
✓ "Calms your mind naturally"
✓ Sip Nature. Sip Wellness.
✗ "Clinically tested formula"
✗ Hyper-technical ingredient copy
🎯
Brand Personality
GlowSip is calm, warm, and purposeful. It speaks to urban wellness seekers aged 22–45 who value natural living and intentional self-care. The brand is approachable but not casual, natural but not rustic.
✓ Empowering and nurturing
✓ Clean and purposeful
✗ Overly playful or loud
✗ Corporate or sterile
